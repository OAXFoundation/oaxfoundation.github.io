  • Hugh Madden
    Hugh Madden,
    Co-founder

    Hugh is responsible for much of the vision behind the OAX project. He has a strong commitment to the future of digital assets and a belief that access should be free and open to all. Hugh started his first business when he was 16, providing security services for companies in Australia.   • Amanda Liu
    Amanda Liu,
    General Manager

    Amanda Liu, General Manager of OAX Foundation, joined in September 2017. She is responsible for building the team and driving the overall strategy and direction for the company. Overseeing the business development, marketing and technology policies for the team, she works closely with internal and external stakeholders, including technology developers, professional consultants and regulatory lawyers.

    Amanda has over 20 years of experience in the banking industry. Prior to joining OAX Foundation, she was Managing Director, Head of Corporate & Institutional Banking at Banco Santander, Hong Kong. Responsible for managing a portfolio of conglomerates and multinantional companies, Amanda was involved with cross-border transactions in Hong Kong, Singapore, Australia, South Korea, UK, Spain, Portugal, Brazil and Argentina. Her previous positions include: Head of Large Corporates at ANZ and building their Hong Kong branch team in Corporate banking; BNP Paribas; Commerzbank; and The Bank of Tokyo-Mitsubishi, including a secondment to Tokyo branch for 10 months. Amanda holds an MBA and Postgraduate Diploma in Professional Accounting from The City University of Hong Kong and a Bachelor of Social Science from The Chinese University of Hong Kong. She was awarded the Asian Banker 'Promising Young Banker' Award in 2011. She also promotes diversity in FinTech industry with speaking engagements in different blockchain and FinTech conferences.

    Amanda Liu
  • Paul Li
    Paul Li,
    General Counsel

    Paul Li is General Counsel to the OAX Foundation. Having joined in the beginning of 2018, Paul oversees all legal and regulatory matters for the team.

    Paul previously worked in financial regulation at a Magic Circle law firm in Hong Kong. There, he advised a broad range of clients on a variety of compliance matters, including local and international regulatory requirements, corporate transactions and the licensing of financial services businesses. Prior to that, Paul worked at a leading Australian law firm and also for the Australian financial services regulator. His experience includes working with and for international regulators on investigations at retail and institutional levels.

    Paul has lectured on regulatory issues at both the University of Sydney and the University of Hong Kong. He received his undergraduate and postgraduate law degrees in Australia.

    Paul Li
  • Wayland Chan

    Wayland Chan is Director of Technology at OAX Foundation. Since joining the team in August 2018, Wayland has been reponsible for developing the OAX project and addressing key technological areas of focus.
