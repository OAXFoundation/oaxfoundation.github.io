Our Mission

Right from the start, our mission has been to build a platform and ecosystem that brings digital asset trading to the mainstream, based on the principles of open, trustlessness and strong governance. As it stands, the majority of decentralized exchanges face significant challenges around speed and scalability. By applying the latest technologies, we have developed a decentralized exchange that overcome these challenges and creates an environment that will allow the mainstream adoption of digital assets to occur. Coupled with partnerships with service providers in areas such as trade surveillance, Know Your Customer (KYC) and, the OAX Project is the stepping stone to help make digital assets an accepted, trusted and valued part of the global financial system.

White Paper

Currently centralized exchanges are opaque, close systems with limited visibility of security and access protocols, while the first generation of decentralized exchanges fail to adequately provide liquidity and trade volume for users. This white paper outlines a clear pathway to a new, significantly improved platform governed by a decentralized autonomous organization.

Our Partners

To bring about change, or to overcome any significant challenges, industry collaboration is key. This is why we have partnered with some of the leading technology companies and innovation labs who share our vision of a digital asset industry based on principles of openness, transparency, decentralization and strong governance. By working together, we will create an environment that brings digital assets to the fore of the global financial system.
